PMSimplex Architecture — Why deterministic first, AI second
The fundamental rule: In O&G and EPC environments, every number in a progress report is a potential evidence point in a contractual dispute. You cannot afford to have those numbers generated by a probabilistic language model. PMSimplex computes EVM, CPM, and float with a deterministic engine — the same input always produces the same output, verifiable by any auditor.
Where AI earns its place: Narrative generation — translating structured data into clear, professional prose — is exactly where LLMs have genuine comparative advantage over engineers. PMSimplex uses AI for the executive summary, discipline narratives, and blocker descriptions only. The numbers that feed those narratives come from the engine, not the model.
The validation gate: No AI-generated content reaches the client without passing through the Planning Engineer's review and the PM's formal approval. PMSimplex makes the boundary explicit in the workflow — the AI generates a draft, a human validates it, and only the validated version carries an official revision index.
